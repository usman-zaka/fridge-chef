Frequently asked questions

These are the most commonly asked questions about FridgeChef.

  • What is FridgeChef?

    FridgeChef is a web application that helps you find recipes based on the ingredients you have. You can search for recipes, save your favorites, and create a virtual fridge with your ingredients to discover recipes that match what you already have.

  • How do I search for recipes?

    You can search for recipes using the search bar on the dashboard page. Simply enter a keyword (like "pasta" or "chicken") to see a list of matching recipes.

  • How do I save a recipe?

    To save a recipe, click the "Save" button on the recipe page. Saved recipes can be viewed in your "Saved Recipes" section for quick access.

  • What is the Virtual Fridge, and how does it work?

    The Virtual Fridge is a feature that lets you add the ingredients you have at home. FridgeChef will then recommend recipes that you can make using these ingredients. You can also search for recipes based on the ingredients in your virtual fridge.

  • How do I add ingredients to my Virtual Fridge?

    Go to the "My Fridge" section, type in the ingredient you want to add and click "Add". You can add as many ingredients as you like.

  • Can I remove ingredients from my Virtual Fridge?

    Yes, you can remove any ingredient by clicking the "X" button next to it in your Virtual Fridge.

  • Do I need an account to use FridgeChef?

    Unfortunately, at the moment we do not support the use of our application without an account. Creating an account allows you to save recipes and maintain your Virtual Fridge for personalized recipe recommendations.
